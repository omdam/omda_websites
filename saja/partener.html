Saudi PartnerTAMER GROUP

Tamer Group is a leading healthcare, beauty care, prestige products, and fast moving consumer goods company responding to the growing needs of the Saudi and Middle East communities. The Group’s core activities are import, distribution, promotion, marketing and manufacturing. Today, Tamer Group has established a special position in the Saudi marketplace, resulting from a unique approach to business, service and customer portfolio.

historyFROM KSA TO MENA

Tamer Group Established in 1922 Tamer Group has been pioneers in bringing modern medicines to Saudi Arabia for over 94 years with annual turnover exceeds $ 1.3 billion. Nowadays is a leading supplier of healthcare products in the MENA region. Its cover the import, marketing and distribution of pharmaceuticals, medical equipment and supplies. Its manufacturing arm “SAJA” produces the latest researched products as well as generics for the MENA, The Group handles the promotion and distribution of a wide range of pharmaceutical products including ethical products, herbal and food supplements.

Japanese StandardsJapanese partners

Together with their Japanese partners, they have invested in the most advanced pharmaceutical production facility yet seen in the Middle East designed and built in strict accordance with Japanese standards, to the highest specifications. SAJA plant is locally manufacturing medicines of a superior quality and exporting them throughout the Middle East and other regions.
