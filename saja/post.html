SAJA licensing and manufacturing cooperation with Sanofi

  • Home
  • Media
  • news
  • SAJA licensing and manufacturing cooperation with Sanofi

10 Feb, 2016 | Posted by Admin

  • 2

SAJA announced signing a contract on 15th December 2015 at SAJA headquarter with Novartis AG to launch SAJA first anti diabetic drug (Jalra® and Jalra M®) as a second brand of Novartis AG blockbuster (Galvus® and Galvus met®).

By signing this agreement, both companies shall be allies in fighting diabetes that affects around 20.6% of adults in Saudi Arabia

The contract also covers a secondary packaging deal of some Novartis AG vital products in SAJA manufacturing facility which reflects the trust in SAJA facilities’ state of the art quality
